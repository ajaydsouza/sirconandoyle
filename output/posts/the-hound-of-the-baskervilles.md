Written in 1902, **The Hound of the Baskervilles** was the third novel (in the series of four) by [Sir Arthur Conan Doyle](/biography-of-sir-arthur-conan-doyle/). Narrated an early case of the dead detective, the murder weapon in the story is an animal.
