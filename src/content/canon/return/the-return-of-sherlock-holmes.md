**The Return of Sherlock Holmes** is a collection of 13 Sherlock Holmes short stories by [Sir Arthur Conan Doyle](/biography-of-sir-arthur-conan-doyle/), published in 1903-1904.

![The Return Of Sherlock Holmes](/images/posts/TheReturnOfSherlockHolmes.jpg)

This is also the first collection after Sherlock Holmes death in [The Final Problem](/oldsite/canon/memoirs/problem/01.php). Faced by public demand, Doyle revived his favorite detective. The very first story in this collection explains his return.

The stories are:

- [The Adventure of the Empty House](/the-adventure-of-the-empty-house/)
- [The Adventure of the Norwood Builder](/the-adventure-of-the-norwood-builder/)
- [The Adventure of the Dancing Men](/the-adventure-of-the-dancing-men/)
- [The Adventure of the Solitary Cyclist](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventure-solitary-cyclist/ "The Adventure of the Solitary Cyclist")
- [The Adventure of the Priory School](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventures-priory-school/ "The Adventures of the Priory School")
- [The Adventure of Black Peter](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventure-black-peter/ "The Adventure of Black Peter")
- [The Adventure of Charles Augustus Milverton](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventure-charles-augustus-milverton/ "The Adventure of Charles Augustus Milverton")
- [The Adventure of the Six Napoleons](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventure-napoleans/ "The Adventure of the Six Napoleans")
- [The Adventure of the Three Students](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventure-students/ "The Adventure of the Three Students")
- [The Adventure of the Golden Prince-Nez](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventure-golden-pince-nez/ "The Adventure of the Golden Pince-nez")
- [The Adventure of the Missing Three-Quarter](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventure-missing-three-quarter/ "The Adventure of the Missing Three-Quarter")
- [The Adventure of the Abbey Grange](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventure-abbey-grange/ "The Adventure of the Abbey Grange")
- [The Adventure of the Second Stain](http://sirconandoyle.com/adventure-stain/ "The Adventure of the Second Stain")
