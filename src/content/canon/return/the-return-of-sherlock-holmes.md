**The Return of Sherlock Holmes** is a collection of 13 Sherlock Holmes short stories by [Sir Arthur Conan Doyle](/biography-of-sir-arthur-conan-doyle/), published in 1903-1904.

![The Return Of Sherlock Holmes](../../../assets/images/posts/TheReturnOfSherlockHolmes.jpg)

This is also the first collection after Sherlock Holmes death in [The Final Problem](/oldsite/canon/memoirs/problem/01.php). Faced by public demand, Doyle revived his favorite detective. The very first story in this collection explains his return.

The stories are:

- [The Adventure of the Empty House](/the-adventure-of-the-empty-house/)
- [The Adventure of the Norwood Builder](/the-adventure-of-the-norwood-builder/)
- [The Adventure of the Dancing Men](/the-adventure-of-the-dancing-men/)
- [The Adventure of the Solitary Cyclist](/adventure-solitary-cyclist/%20%22The%20Adventure%20of%20the%20Solitary%20Cyclist%22/)
- [The Adventure of the Priory School](/adventures-priory-school/%20%22The%20Adventures%20of%20the%20Priory%20School%22/)
- [The Adventure of Black Peter](/adventure-black-peter/%20%22The%20Adventure%20of%20Black%20Peter%22/)
- [The Adventure of Charles Augustus Milverton](/adventure-charles-augustus-milverton/%20%22The%20Adventure%20of%20Charles%20Augustus%20Milverton%22/)
- [The Adventure of the Six Napoleons](/adventure-napoleans/%20%22The%20Adventure%20of%20the%20Six%20Napoleans%22/)
- [The Adventure of the Three Students](/adventure-students/%20%22The%20Adventure%20of%20the%20Three%20Students%22/)
- [The Adventure of the Golden Prince-Nez](/adventure-golden-pince-nez/%20%22The%20Adventure%20of%20the%20Golden%20Pince-nez%22/)
- [The Adventure of the Missing Three-Quarter](/adventure-missing-three-quarter/%20%22The%20Adventure%20of%20the%20Missing%20Three-Quarter%22/)
- [The Adventure of the Abbey Grange](/adventure-abbey-grange/%20%22The%20Adventure%20of%20the%20Abbey%20Grange%22/)
- [The Adventure of the Second Stain](/adventure-stain/%20%22The%20Adventure%20of%20the%20Second%20Stain%22/)
