## What personal data we collect and why

### Hosting and infrastructure

This site is served via **Cloudflare Workers** and **GitHub Pages**. When you visit any page, your request passes through Cloudflare's network. Cloudflare may log standard server data (IP address, browser user agent, pages visited, timestamps) for security, performance, and abuse prevention purposes. Cloudflare also collects aggregate traffic analytics (requests, bandwidth, error rates) visible to the site owner. This is handled by Cloudflare under their own [Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

### Cookies

This site does not set any cookies itself. However, Cloudflare — our infrastructure provider — may set cookies (such as `__cf_bm` or `cf_clearance`) for bot detection and security purposes. See [Cloudflare's cookie policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/cookie-policy/) for details.
